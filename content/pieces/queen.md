# Queen

The **Queen** is a combined line piece (rook + bishop). Unless modified by civilization/research, it follows standard queen movement.

## Summary
- **Moves:** any number of squares orthogonally or diagonally.
- **Captures:** by landing on the target square.
- **Jumping:** cannot jump over pieces.

## Movement
A Queen may move any number of empty squares:
- horizontally,
- vertically, or
- diagonally.

## Capturing
A Queen captures by moving onto an enemy-occupied square along a valid queen line.

## Common modifiers (research / civ effects)
- **Dame (Research):** queens gain knight-style movement. :contentReference[oaicite:32]{index=32}
- **Spanish:** under the stated board-control condition, knights become queens. :contentReference[oaicite:33]{index=33}
