# Queen

The Queen is classified as a [major piece](#/pieces/index#major) with the following properties:

- **Moves:** any number of squares orthogonally or diagonally.
- **Captures:** by landing on the target square.
- **Jumping:** cannot jump over [pieces](#/pieces/index).

<br>
---

## Research Modifiers
**[Dame](#/research/dame):** Queens gain [knight](#/pieces/knight) movement.  
**[Morganatic Marriage](#/research/morganatic-marriage):** [Minor pieces](#/pieces/index#minor) cannot be promoted into queens.  
**[Sneaky Queen](#/research/sneaky-queen):** One random [minor piece](#/pieces/index#minor) or [horseman](#/pieces/horseman) becomes a hidden queen, revealing after its first non-standard move.

## Civilization Interactions
**[French](#/civilizations/french):**
- When your [King](#/pieces/king) dies, buff all other [pieces](#/pieces/index) for 3 turns. Your queen gains [knight](#/pieces/knight) movement during this buff window.
**[Huns](#/civilizations/huns):** 
- Replace all [non-royal pieces](#/pieces/royals) with [horseman](#/pieces/horseman).
**[Spanish](#/civilizations/spanish):**
- While you have more total [pieces](#/pieces/index) on the 6th, 7th, and 8th ranks than your opponent, all of your [knights](#/pieces/knight) become queens.
