# Pawn

The Pawn is classified as a [minor piece](#/pieces/index#minor) with the following properties:

- **Moves:** 1 square forward.
- **Captures:** 1 square diagonally forward by landing on the target square.
- **Jumping:** cannot jump over [pieces](#/pieces/index).

---

## Research Modifiers
**[Tactical Retreat](#/research/tactical-retreat):** [Minor pieces](#/pieces/index#minor) may move one square straight backward.
**[Scorched Earth](#/research/scorched-earth):** Every 10th turn, one random [minor piece](#/pieces/index#minor) or [horseman](#/pieces/horseman) is removed from the board.
**[Junshi](#/research/junshi):** When a [minor piece](#/pieces/index#minor) dies, any friendly [minor piece](#/pieces/index#minor) behind it in the same file dies in solidarity.
**[Morganatic Marriage](#/research/morganatic-marriage):** [Minor pieces](#/pieces/index#minor) cannot be promoted into [queens](#/pieces/queen).
**[Fletching](#/research/Fletching):** [Minor pieces](#/pieces/index#minor) do not move when capturing pieces. 
**[Sneaky Queen](#/research/sneaky-queen):** One random [minor piece](#/pieces/index#minor) or [horseman](#/pieces/horseman) becomes a hidden [queen](#/pieces/queen), revealing after its first non-standard move.
**[Blitzkrieg](#/research/blitzkrieg):** During the first three turns, the same [minor pieces](#/pieces/index#minor) must move twice each turn.

## Civilization Interactions
**[Britons](#/civilizations/britons):** 
- Your [pawns](#/pieces/pawn) do not move when capturing.
**[Burgundians](#/civilizations/burgundians):** 
- Upon reaching the **6th** or **7th** (for black: **3rd** or **2nd**) rank, your [pawns](#/pieces/pawn) ***may*** be promoted into a [knight](#/pieces/knight).
**[Chinese](#/civilizations/chinese):**
- Start with 2 additional pawns on the **d3** and **e3** (for black: **d6** and **e6**) square.
- If your [Emperor](#/pieces/emperor) dies and you have 2 or more pawns left, promote a pawn into a new [Emperor](#/pieces/emperor) (this does not use your turn).
**[French](#/civilizations/french):**
- When your [King](#/pieces/king) dies, buff all other [pieces](#/pieces/index) for 3 turns. Your pawn gains [king](#/pieces/king) movement during this buff window.
**[Huns](#/civilizations/huns):** 
- all [non-royal pieces](#/pieces/index#royals) are replaced with [horseman](#/pieces/horseman).
**[Japanese](#/civilizations/japanese):** 
- All of your [pawns](#/pieces/pawn) are replaced with [ninjas](#/pieces/ninja).
**[Mongols](#/civilizations/mongols):**
- If your pawns capture a [non-minor piece](#/pieces/index#minor), they are promoted to a [knight](#/pieces/knight).
**[Spanish](#/civilizations/spanish):**
- While you have more total [pieces](#/pieces/index) on the **6th**, **7th**, and **8th**(for black: **3rd**, **2nd**, and **1st**) ranks then your opponent, your pawns become [knights](#/pieces/knight).
**[Teutons](#/civilizations/teutons):**
- Enemy pawns cannot capture your pawns for the first **9** turns.
**[Vikings](#/civilizations/vikings):**
- Your pawns gain the ability to attack directly in front of them.
- If an enemy pawn takes a [Viking](#/civilizations/vikings) [major piece](#/pieces/index#major), the enemy pawn also dies.
