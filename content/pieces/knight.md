# Knight

The **Knight** is a jumping piece. Unless modified by civilization/research, it follows standard knight movement.

## Summary
- **Moves:** standard knight “L” pattern.
- **Captures:** by landing on the target square.
- **Jumping:** can jump over pieces (standard knight behavior).

## Movement
A Knight moves in an “L” shape:
- 2 squares in one direction, then
- 1 square perpendicular.

## Capturing
A Knight captures by landing on an enemy-occupied square that is a valid knight destination.

## Common modifiers
- **Burgundians:** knights can also move 1 square left/right/forward/back. :contentReference[oaicite:28]{index=28}
- **Mongols:** knights can move 1 square diagonally. :contentReference[oaicite:29]{index=29}
- **Horse Shoes (Research):** knights gain a one-square diagonal movement option. :contentReference[oaicite:30]{index=30}
- **Lancers (Research):** knights can charge 2 squares straight forward. :contentReference[oaicite:31]{index=31}
