# King

The **King** is the royal piece in most modes/civilizations. Unless a mode says otherwise, it uses standard king movement.

## Summary
- **Moves:** 1 square in any direction.
- **Captures:** by landing on the target square.
- **Royal rules:** may be affected by gamemode (example: Regicide). :contentReference[oaicite:35]{index=35}

## Movement
A King moves 1 square in any direction (orthogonal or diagonal).

## Capturing
A King captures by moving onto an adjacent enemy-occupied square.

## Gamemode note
- **Regicide:** you win by capturing the King (not checkmate) and you do not have to defend a check. :contentReference[oaicite:36]{index=36}

## Civilization interactions
- **Chinese:** King is replaced with an Emperor (see Emperor page). :contentReference[oaicite:37]{index=37}
- **Egyptians:** King is replaced with a Pharaoh. :contentReference[oaicite:38]{index=38}
