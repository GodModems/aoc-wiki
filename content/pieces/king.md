# King

The King is classified as a [royal piece](#/pieces/index#royals) with the following properties:

- **Moves:** 1 square in any direction.
- **Captures:** by landing on the target square.
- **Jumping:** cannot jump over [pieces](#/pieces/index).

<br>
---

## Research Modifiers
**[Feudalism](#/research/feudalism):** [Royals](#/pieces/index#royals) may move 2 squares forward instead of only one.
**[Castle 2](#/research/castle-2):** [Royals](#/pieces/index#royals) may swap places with any friendly [piece](#/pieces/index) once per game. 
**[Emergency Coronations](#/research/emergency-coronations):** When a King dies, a random friendly [knight](#/pieces/knight) is crowned the new King. 
**[Noble Sacrifice](#/research/noble-sacrifice):** When checked by exactly one [piece](#/pieces/index), sacrifice all [minor pieces](#/pieces/index#minor) to capture it.

<br>
---

## Civilization Interactions
**[Chinese](#/civilizations/chinese):** 
- Your King is replaced with an [Emperor](#/pieces/emperor).
**[Egyptians](#/civilizations/egyptians):**
- Your King is replaced with a [Pharaoh](#/pieces/pharaoh).
**[French](#/civilizations/french):**
- When your King dies, buff all other [pieces](#/pieces/index) for 3 turns. If you do not win in those 3 turns, you lose.
