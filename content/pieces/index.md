## Piece types

Pieces are generally placed into 1 of 3 categories:

<br>

### Minor
The frontline backbone. Minor pieces are plentiful, evolve through promotion rules, and define most opening structures.

- [Pawn](#/pieces/pawn)
- [Ninja](#/pieces/ninja)

<br>

### Major
More complex and valuable. These pieces provide reach, tactics, and win conditions beyond pawn structure.

- [Bishop](#/pieces/bishop)
- [Horseman](#/pieces/horseman)
- [Knight](#/pieces/knight)
- [Queen](#/pieces/queen)

<br>

### Royals
High-value leadership pieces. Losing your royal is usually decisive and often interacts with lose conditions.

- [King](#/pieces/king)
- [Emperor](#/pieces/emperor)
- [Pharaoh](#/pieces/pharaoh)
