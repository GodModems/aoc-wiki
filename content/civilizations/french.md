## Rules
When the French [King](#/pieces/king) dies, all [pieces](#/pieces/index) are buffed for **3 turns**:
- [Queen](#/pieces/queen): gain **[Knight](#/pieces/knight)** movement
- [Rook](#/pieces/rook): gain **[Bishop](#/pieces/bishop)** movement
- [Knight](#/pieces/knight): gain **[Queen](#/pieces/queen)** movement
- [Bishop](#/pieces/bishop): gain **[Rook](#/pieces/rook)** movement
- [Pawn](#/pieces/pawn): gain **[King](#/pieces/king)** movement

Win/Lose condition after the [King](#/pieces/king) dies:
- **Lose** if your opponent survives those 3 turns.
- **Win** if you checkmate your opponent before the 3 turns end.
