# French

## Rules
When the French King dies, all pieces are buffed for **3 turns**:
- Queen: gain **Knight** movement
- Rook: gain **Bishop** movement
- Knight: gain **Queen** movement
- Bishop: gain **Rook** movement
- Pawn: gain **King** movement

Win/Lose condition after the King dies:
- If the enemy survives those 3 turns, the **French lose** the game.
- The French **win** if they **checkmate** their opponent before the 3 turns end.
