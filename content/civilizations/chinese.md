
# Chinese

## Rules
- Start with **2 extra pawns** on **d3** and **e3** (for Black: **d6** and **e6**).
  - These extra pawns do **not** get the normal 2-square first move.
- Your **King is replaced with an Emperor**.
- If the Emperor dies and you have **2 or more pawns**, convert a pawn into a **new Emperor**.
- You **lose** when you have **no Emperor**.
