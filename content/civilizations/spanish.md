# Spanish

## Rules
While you have more total pieces on the **6th, 7th, and 8th** ranks (for Black: **3rd, 2nd, and 1st**) than your opponent, gain the following buffs:
- All of your Knights become **Queens**.
- All of your Pawns become **Knights**.
