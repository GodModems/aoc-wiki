# Huns
The Huns were a formidable nomadic confederation that surged westward across the steppes, becoming a fearsome presence on the fringes of late Rome. Known for swift horse warfare and shock raids, they reached their peak under Attila, pressuring empires and accelerating the upheavals that transformed Europe in late antiquity.
